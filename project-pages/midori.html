Midori – Matcha Ratio
Personal Project / Product Design & Development
Midori is a minimalist companion app I designed and built to take the guesswork out of preparing ceremonial-grade matcha. The experience centers on a single interaction: pick your matcha grade, enter how much powder you have, and instantly get the ideal water ratio with friendly brewing guidance.
I crafted the entire product in SwiftUI with a focus on typography, clarity, and accessible defaults. The app ships with haptic feedback, dynamic type, dark mode, and localized measurements so it feels right at home on every device. Midori launched on the App Store in September 2024 and is completely free—no accounts, no ads, just delightful tea rituals.
Role
Solo designer & iOS engineer—from concept sketches to shipping build.
Tech Stack
SwiftUI, Observation framework, AppStorage for persistence, SF Symbols, custom design tokens.
Launch Snapshot
Featured screens for Ceremonial, Premium, and Culinary matcha with precise ratios and brewing notes.
