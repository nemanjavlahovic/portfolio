Car:Go Anywhere
Project Details / Background
Car:Go Anywhere is a leading ride-hailing application in Serbia, designed to provide users with a seamless and reliable transportation experience. As a key developer on this project, I focused on creating a user-friendly interface that allows users to easily book rides, track their drivers in real-time, and enjoy safe, comfortable transportation.
The app incorporates features such as real-time GPS tracking, transparent pricing, and secure payment options. My role involved implementing these core functionalities, ensuring smooth performance across various devices, and maintaining the app's intuitive design. I also worked on integrating map services and optimizing the app for efficient route calculation and driver matching.
