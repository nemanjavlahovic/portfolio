HapticLabs Player
Project Details / Background
HapticLabs Player is a cutting-edge iOS application that revolutionizes the way users experience music. As a lead developer on this project, I focused on creating a unique audio player that incorporates haptic feedback technology to enhance the listening experience.
The app analyzes music in real-time and generates synchronized haptic patterns that users can feel through their devices. I implemented complex algorithms for audio analysis and haptic pattern generation, ensuring seamless integration with iOS's haptic feedback capabilities. The challenge was to create a responsive and power-efficient system that could provide an immersive experience without draining the device's battery.
