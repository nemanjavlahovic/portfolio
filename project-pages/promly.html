Promly for High School
Project Details / Background
Promly for High School is an innovative social platform specifically designed to address the unique needs of high school students. As a lead iOS developer on this project, I focused on creating a safe, engaging, and supportive digital environment for teens to connect, collaborate, and access vital mental health resources.
The app features a range of functionalities including peer-to-peer messaging, group discussions, collaborative project spaces, and a curated library of mental health resources. I implemented robust privacy and safety measures to ensure a secure environment for young users. A key challenge was balancing user engagement with digital well-being, incorporating features like usage reminders and mood tracking to promote healthy online habits.
