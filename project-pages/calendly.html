Calendly Mobile
Project Details / Background
Calendly Mobile is an iOS application designed to streamline scheduling processes for professionals. As a key developer on this project, I focused on creating a user-friendly interface that allows users to manage their schedules efficiently, set their availability, and connect with clients or colleagues seamlessly.
The app integrates with various calendar services and provides features such as customizable event types, automated reminders, and timezone detection. My role involved implementing these features, ensuring smooth performance, and maintaining the app's intuitive design across different iOS devices.
