Calendly Mobile
Project Details / Background
Calendly Mobile is the official iOS companion app for the popular scheduling platform. During my short-term contract with Calendly, I worked specifically on implementing the "Schedule in Real Time" feature, which allows users to instantly book and manage meetings on the go.
This feature required deep integration with calendar APIs, real-time synchronization, and careful attention to performance optimization to ensure a smooth user experience. I collaborated with the existing team to deliver this functionality while maintaining consistency with Calendly's design standards and existing codebase.
