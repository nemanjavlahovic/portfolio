Included Health
Project Details / Background
Included Health is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to revolutionize how users access and manage their healthcare. As a senior iOS developer on this project, I played a crucial role in creating a user-friendly and feature-rich mobile application that seamlessly integrates various healthcare services.
The app connects users with top doctors, provides personalized care guidance, and helps manage health benefits all in one place. I implemented key features such as secure video consultations, appointment scheduling, health record management, and personalized health recommendations. The challenge was to create a robust, HIPAA-compliant application that could handle sensitive health data while providing a smooth and intuitive user experience.
