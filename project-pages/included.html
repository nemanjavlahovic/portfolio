Included Health
Project Details / Background
Role: Senior iOS Developer (Contract via Vicert)
Included Health is a comprehensive healthcare platform connecting users with top doctors and personalized care. As part of Vicert's team of 30 skilled iOS developers, I worked on building and maintaining critical healthcare features for both providers and patients using SwiftUI and modern iOS technologies.
Following a company merger, I led a comprehensive UIKit application refactoring project, spearheading process enhancements and orchestrating the transition to SwiftUI. This involved revitalizing and upgrading all aspects of the application while ensuring HIPAA compliance and maintaining the highest standards for handling sensitive health data. The contract role required close collaboration with cross-functional teams to deliver a robust, scalable healthcare solution.
