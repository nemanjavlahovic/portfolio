My Background

I'm a senior iOS developer based in Barcelona with 8+ years of experience building native applications for startups and enterprises. My work spans healthcare, transportation, and social platforms, with a focus on creating performant, maintainable codebases that scale.

I specialize in Swift, SwiftUI, and UIKit, with extensive experience in complex UI implementations, API integrations, and app architecture. I've led development on apps serving millions of users, optimized legacy codebases, and built MVPs from scratch.

I value direct communication, clean code, and shipping quality products on time. Whether you need to build a new product, scale an existing app, or modernize your iOS codebase, I can help you deliver a solution that works for both your users and your business.
